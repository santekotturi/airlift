📦 Airlift

Your Fitbit sleep, in Apple Health.

Last night's sleep — with full stages — plus heart rate, HRV, SpO₂, steps and more, carried from your Fitbit into Apple Health. Every morning. On your iPhone.

On-device · open source · no account, no server
How to get it View source

For Fitbit Air and other Google-account Fitbit devices.

One place for everything you track

The Fitbit Air keeps your data in Google's world, and it doesn't write to Apple Health on its own. Airlift bridges the gap.

😴

Full sleep stages

Awake, light, deep and REM — mapped faithfully, plus time in bed.

💓

Your vitals

Heart rate, resting HR, HRV, SpO₂ and respiratory rate.

🚶

Activity

Steps and distance, on the days your band recorded them.

✅

Checked first

Every reading is compared to what Apple Health already has. Auto-import the clean ones, or review everything yourself.

🔒

Private by design

Runs entirely on your iPhone. Your Google sign-in stays in the Keychain. No analytics, no ads, no server.

🌎

Travel-correct

A night logged in one time zone lands on the right day, wherever you sync.
